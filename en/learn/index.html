Understanding medicinal mushroom extraction

Medicinal mushroom extraction is a complex process where different solvents and physical conditions are used to isolate distinct groups of bioactive compounds.

Simple extraction methods often fail to achieve full spectrum extraction. In practice, multi-stage approaches such as triple extraction, ultrasonic assistance and low-temperature vacuum concentration are required.

This section explains the processes behind GoMushroom tinctures and highlights why process control, standardization and quality control are critical for the final product.
