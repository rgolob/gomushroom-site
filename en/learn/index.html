Knowledge about medicinal mushroom extraction

A practical explanation of medicinal mushroom extraction from the perspective of real production, not just theory.

Medicinal mushroom extraction is a complex process in which different solvents and physical conditions are used to obtain different groups of bioactive compounds. Simple methods often do not allow full extraction efficiency, which is why multi-stage approaches such as triple extraction, ultrasonic assistance and low-temperature vacuum concentration are used in practice.

This page brings together explanations of the processes we use in the development of extracts and GoMushroom tinctures, and explains why process control, standardization and quality control are important for the final product.
