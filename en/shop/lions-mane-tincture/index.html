GoMushroom Lion's Mane tincture is a dietary supplement made from the fruiting bodies of Hericium erinaceus. It is prepared as a concentrated extract in liquid form.

It is intended for people looking for a traceable, carefully prepared extract with a clear source of raw material and a controlled process.

The raw material for Lion's Mane comes from Slovenia, from Pohorska gobarna. It's not a generic imported extract — the raw material is grown by us with a clear origin.

Purified water, Hericium erinaceus mushroom extract, ethanol or vegetable glycerin, sunflower lecithin, vitamin C.

10–20 drops, 2–3 times daily. The tincture can be taken sublingually or diluted in a small amount of water or juice.

Shake the bottle well before use.

The alcohol-based version contains ethanol as part of the final formulation. The alcohol-free version is prepared from the same concentrated extract base, with ethanol and part of the water removed after extraction, then vegetable glycerin added to reach the same final volume.

Because of this, the content of extracted compounds is comparable between both versions.

Concentrated tinctures may naturally show some cloudiness, sediment or slight fraction separation. This is a normal occurrence in products with a higher content of extracted substances.

GoMushroom places emphasis on its own raw material, small batches, process control and testing of the final product.

Independent heavy metal testing is carried out on the final concentrated product.
