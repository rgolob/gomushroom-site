Not just Reishi. The process matters.

The quality of a tincture doesn't depend only on the name of the mushroom, but on the raw material, the extraction, the concentration and the final formulation.

Multi-stage extraction

Reishi contains compounds with different solubility. That's why we use a combination of alcohol-based and water-based extraction stages.

Transparent quality

The final product is tested for heavy metals, since extraction concentrates both the desired compounds and any unwanted trace elements.

Process

Why triple extraction?

Reishi contains various groups of natural compounds. Some are extracted more effectively in ethanol, others in water. A single extraction step therefore can't capture the full complexity of the raw material.

At GoMushroom, the tincture is made through a multi-stage process: alcohol extraction, hot-water extraction and an additional water extraction under different conditions.

The point of this approach isn't a marketing claim of being "stronger," but a more deliberate and broader extraction profile.

Quality

From raw material to finished product

GoMushroom tinctures are not designed as a repackaged industrial extract. The process includes our own extract preparation, concentration of individual fractions and final formulation.

This approach allows for more control over what actually ends up in the bottle — not just in terms of composition, but also traceability, stability and quality.

GoMushroom Reishi tincture is a dietary supplement made from the fruiting bodies of Ganoderma lucidum. It is prepared as a concentrated extract in liquid form.

The product is intended for people looking for a traceable, carefully made and process-controlled medicinal mushroom extract.

Purified water, Ganoderma lucidum mushroom extract, ethanol or vegetable glycerin, sunflower lecithin, vitamin C.

10–20 drops, 2–3 times daily. The tincture can be taken sublingually or diluted in a small amount of water or juice.

Shake the bottle well before use.

The alcohol-based version contains ethanol as part of the final formulation. The alcohol-free version is prepared from the same concentrated extract base, with ethanol and part of the water removed after extraction, then vegetable glycerin added to reach the same final volume.

Because of this, the content of extracted compounds is comparable between both versions.

Concentrated tinctures may naturally show some cloudiness, sediment or slight fraction separation. This is a normal occurrence in products with a higher content of extracted substances.

GoMushroom places emphasis on its own raw material, small batches, process control and testing of the final product.

Independent heavy metal testing is carried out on the final concentrated product.
