Approach — triple extraction & vacuum concentration

We push each mushroom to its maximum — repeatably, traceably, and gently.

Step 1

Triple extraction

Ethanol → hot-water → pressure-water. Each phase targets a different compound spectrum, resulting in a full-range extract.

Step 2

High solvent volume

Higher solvent volume prevents early saturation, maintains mass transfer, and increases overall extraction yield.

Step 3

Ultrasound support

Ultrasound microcavitation loosens the mushroom matrix, speeding up extraction and increasing yield.

Step 4

Vacuum concentration

Solvents are removed at lower temperatures, preserving sensitive compounds and retaining natural aroma and color.

QC 1

Quality

Internal QC on raw materials and finished products: dry matter, density, extractable fraction, and batch traceability.

QC 2

Compliance

Work follows HACCP principles and a GMP approach; ethanol use is documented in line with excise records.
